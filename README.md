Amazon Sales Performance & Order Analytics Dashboard

Built an end-to-end eCommerce Sales Analytics dashboard analyzing:
📦 1,16,649 Total Orders
💰 ₹7,85,92,678 Total Sales Amount
🛍 7,190 Products across 9 Categories
📏 11 Product Sizes across multiple categories

Objective:
Analyze sales performance, product demand patterns, logistics efficiency, and regional sales distribution to generate actionable insights that support data-driven decision-making for eCommerce operations.

What I Built
Cleaned and structured the Amazon sales dataset in Excel
Validated order-level records and standardized fields
Created calculated metrics such as Total Quantity, Total Sales Amount, Product Count, and Category Count
Built weekly sales and order trend analysis across categories
Developed courier status distribution analysis to evaluate fulfillment efficiency
Performed sales channel comparison between Amazon and Non-Amazon orders
Designed state-wise sales distribution using geographic mapping
Analyzed product demand patterns by size and category
Built Top 10 state performance analysis with shipping type comparison (Expedited vs Standard)
Developed an interactive Tableau Executive Dashboard with dynamic category filtering

Key Insights:
Sales Overview
Total Orders Processed: 1,16,649
Total Sales Revenue Generated: ₹7.85+ Crore

Fulfillment Performance
94.21% of orders were successfully shipped
Cancelled orders represent a very small portion of total orders

Sales Channel Insights
Amazon.in contributes ~99.86% of total orders
Non-Amazon channels contribute only ~0.14%

Regional Sales Distribution
Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Tamil Nadu are the top performing states by quantity
Southern and western regions show stronger demand concentration

Product Size Demand
Medium (M) and Large (L) sizes show the highest demand
Larger sizes like 4XL–6XL contribute minimal order volume

Weekly Sales Trends
Peak sales observed around Week 16 and Week 24
Sales fluctuations indicate promotional or seasonal demand patterns
